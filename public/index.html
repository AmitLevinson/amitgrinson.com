Bio

Hi, I'm Amit Levinson, I'm a M.A student for Sociology & Anthropology at Ben-Gurion University. I live with my fiance at Be'er Sheva in southern Israel. My interests include my thesis topic, drug markets and technology, other fields like political participation, statistics and R. I currently TA ‘introduction to statistics’ in the S & A department, and am a research assistant to Dr. Jennifer Oser from the political science department researching online and offline political participation.

I opened this website for several reasons: (1) I can have a reason to blog and challenge myself with new R analysis and visulizations; (2) So that i can share what and how I analyze for others to use.
