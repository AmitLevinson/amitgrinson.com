Bio

Hi, I'm Amit Levinson, I'm an MA student for Sociology & Anthropology at Ben-Gurion University of the Negev. I currently live with my fiancé in Be'er Sheva, southern Israel. My interests include my thesis topic, drug markets and technology, other fields such as political participation, statistics and R. In my daily job I am a teaching assistant for ‘introduction to statistics’ in the Sociology & Anthropology Department and work as a research assistant for Dr. Jennifer Oser from the Political Science Department researching online and offline political participation.

I opened this website so that I can have a reason to blog and challenge myself with new R analysis and visulizations; and to share what and how I analyze for others to use. Enjoy!
